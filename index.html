3. Automatic Vehicle Parking System. (Major project)
An automatic vehicle parking model , using Arduino Microcontroller , IR Sensor and Python framework ,embedded through Serial module. It works in a way such that when a vehicle enters the parking lot ,the sensors command the camera to capture the frame. The installed camera captures the number plate of vehicle and record this data including time in database. If there is atleast one parking lot is vacant ,then only the entry gate will open and it eventually update the slot availabilty on the screen ,otherwise the gate won't open and the message will display that "NO SLOTS LEFT" . In the exit system , when a particular vehicle want to exit ,the sensors again detect the vehicle and command the camera to capture the frame , if the vehicle number matches the record in the database , the system will display total duration and generates a QR code for the payment process. The exit gate will open only after the consumer scan and pays the amount. After the vehicle leaves the parking arena , the slots will update itself again.
